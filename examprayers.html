Common Questions

When should I book the seva — before or on the day of NEET?
Most parents book 2–7 days before the exam so the seva is completed and the blessings reach the student before they sit for the paper. You can also book the night before — we'll perform it at the earliest sunrise.
Can I book both sevas together?
Yes — you can submit the form twice (once for each seva), or call us and we'll combine both into one ₹402 enrollment with both proofs delivered on the same day.
Does my child need to be present?
No. The seva is performed in the student's name with a sankalp. Your child can simply focus on their preparation and the exam.
Will this guarantee my child clears NEET?
We are honest with you — no seva, prayer, or person on earth can guarantee an exam result. What we offer is a way to channel your love and worry into a real, time-honoured act of faith. The hard work has been done by your child. This is your blessing.
How will I receive the proof?
Photos and a short video are sent on WhatsApp to the mobile number you provided. For Mahakal Prarthana, you'll also receive a prasad image and the pandit's name and contact for verification.
